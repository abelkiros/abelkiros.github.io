Welcome!

I am a Tigrayan-American born in Rochester, New York. My educational journey commenced at Monroe Community College and later led me to the esteemed Rochester Institute of Technology, where my passion for technology and creativity blossomed. As an aspiring cybersecurity professional, I am fascinated by the limitless opportunities that technology offers.

My aim is to fortify digital landscapes against threats, specializing in penetration testing, network security, and risk assessment. Concurrently, I am refining my software skills to pioneer innovative solutions that redefine our interaction with technology.

Beyond the digital realm, I derive immense joy from the camaraderie and excitement that sports bring. When not engrossed in athletics, I express my creativity through various art forms—sketching, painting, or experimenting with digital designs. Exploring diverse cultures and their intricacies captivates me; I relish learning about different traditions, languages, and customs, fostering a deeper understanding of our wonderfully diverse world.

Within this myriad of interests, delving into my Ethiopian heritage holds a special place. Understanding its rich history, traditions, and values is a deeply personal journey that further enriches my understanding of both myself and the world around me.

Sports remain a steadfast presence in my life. Whether experiencing the camaraderie of a game or relishing the focus of a solitary run, sports keep me energized and grounded. My aspirations transcend personal growth; I am driven to inspire and empower. Striving to uplift my friends, family, and community, I aim to share knowledge, foster ambition, and encourage everyone to reach greater heights.
