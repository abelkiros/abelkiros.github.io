Welcome!

I am a Tigrayan-American, born in Rochester, New York. As an aspiring cybersecurity professional, I am fascinated by the endless opportunities that technology provides. I aim to strengthen digital landscapes against threats, focusing on penetration testing, network security, and risk assessment. At the same time, I am honing my software skills to create innovative solutions that transform our interaction with technology. Beyond the digital world, I find great joy in the camaraderie and thrill that sports offer. When not immersed in athletics, I express my creativity through art forms such as sketching, painting, and experimenting with digital designs. I am captivated by exploring diverse cultures and their complexities. I enjoy learning about different traditions, languages, and customs, which deepens my understanding of our diverse world. Among my wide range of interests, exploring my Ethiopian heritage holds a special place. Learning about its rich history, traditions, and values is a deeply personal journey that enriches my understanding of myself and the world around me. My aspirations go beyond personal growth - I am motivated to inspire and empower others. I aim to uplift my friends, family, and community by sharing knowledge, fostering ambition, and encouraging everyone to strive for greater heights.
